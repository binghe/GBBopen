awaken-process

awaken-process   process[Function]

Purpose
Awaken a hibernating process.

Package   :portable-threads

Module   :portable-threads

Arguments

process     A process

Errors
Multiprocessing is not supported on the Common Lisp implementation.
Lisp process scheduling is not available with SBCL threads.

See also
    hibernate-process

Example 

  (awaken-process process)

Notes
Hibernate-process is heavy handed and can lead to deadlocks and timing artifacts. Support for hibernate-process and awaken-process may be removed, and their use is not recommended.
