do-until

do-until   form test-form[Macro]

Purpose
Evaluates form and then test-form repeatedly, as long as test-form evaluates to nil.

Package   :gbbopen-tools

Module   :gbbopen-tools

Arguments

form     A form
test-form     A form

See also
    until
    while

Examples 

> (let ((i 0)) 
    (do-until (print i)
        (> (incf i) 3)))
1 
2 
3 
nil
> (let ((i 10)) 
    (do-until (print i)
        (> (incf i) 3)))
10
nil
